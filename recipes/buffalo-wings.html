Baked Buffalo Wings

Buffalo wings made in the oven rather than deep fried.



Ingredients:

  • Wings
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Wings
  • Hot Sauce
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Apply the seasoning to the wings
  2. Bake the wings
  3. Prepare the sauce in a pan with butter added
  4. Add the sauce to the baked wings, tossing in a bowl
  5. Enjoy!
